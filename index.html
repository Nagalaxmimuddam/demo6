Boutique

A boutique is a small shopping outlet, especially one that specializes in elite and fashionable items such as clothing and jewellery. The word is French for "shop". "Boutique" was an everyday English word in the late 1960s when, for a brief period, Swinging London was the centre of the fashion trade.

About us

A boutique is a small shop that sells fashionable clothing, accessories, or other goods to a specific market. The word "boutique" comes from the French word for "shop". Here are some things to know about boutiques: Types of boutiques: Boutiques can be luxury, boho, or high-end fashion boutiques. Boutique services: Boutiques may offer personal shopping, alterations, and style assessments. Boutique business requirements: To start a boutique, you'll need to obtain the required licenses and permits, including a business license, sales tax permit, and zoning permits. You may also need health permits if you sell food or beverages, a signage permit, a music license, and a fire department permit.
